About Service

BTD MailMaster provides enterprise-grade disposable email infrastructure.

Features:
• Maintain up to 1000 active IDs simultaneously.
• Exclusive premium domain .
• Real-time Gmail Forwarding.
• Push notifications & HTML Viewer.
Engineered for ultimate privacy and speed.

User Tutorial
  • Generate: Use the '+' button to create a single or series of IDs.
  • Manage: Go to 'Active Registry' to view, copy, or delete them.
  • Forwarding: Link your personal Gmail to auto-forward incoming mails.
  • Inbox: Tap the mail icon to view real-time OTPs and messages safely.
Terms & Conditions
1. Service Overview
BTD MailMaster provides temporary email addresses for verification and testing purposes. We do not offer long-term storage.

2. Data Retention & Liability
This platform operates on a transient data model. We hold absolutely no responsibility for the retention, security, or backup of your received emails. All messages and generated IDs are temporary and may be permanently wiped from our servers at any time without prior notice.

3. Acceptable Use
Users must not utilize this service for illicit, fraudulent, or harmful activities. We reserve the right to revoke access if abuse is detected.
